About Christopher Carazas

I WRITE ABOUT WHAT
HAPPENS AFTER SURVIVAL.

Christopher Carazas is an author and social impact measurement analyst based in Kingston, Massachusetts, writing about autism, grief, belonging, survival, and the lives we rebuild after collapse.

Author. Analyst. Reluctant specialist in starting over.

Christopher Carazas grew up across Ecuador, Bolivia, Paraguay, Ghana, and Senegal, later living and working in Oman, Switzerland, and Madagascar. He writes about late-diagnosed autism, psychological abuse, grief, belonging, and the distance between surviving and feeling alive.

He is also a social impact measurement analyst developing tools that connect stories, outcomes, and measurable value. Shadow, his German Shepherd, serves as companion, editor, and a generally hostile reviewer of human systems.
